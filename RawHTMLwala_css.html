About Dengue Fever

Dengue Fever is a viral disease spread by mosquito bites. It is common in tropical countries.

Causes

  • Mosquito bites
  • Standing water where mosquitoes breed

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Headache
  • Muscle and joint pain
  • Skin rash

Prevention

  • Remove standing water
  • Use mosquito repellent
  • Wear long sleeves

Treatment

Rest and drink fluids. Seek medical help if symptoms get worse.

About Tuberculosis

Tuberculosis is a bacterial infection that mainly affects the lungs.

Causes

  • Airborne bacteria

Symptoms
